UX & UI Responsive Design

DOG IS MY COPILOT

Built to bridge the gap between road trip apps and apps for dog owners, Dog Is My Copilot provides dog friendly locations and road trip recommendations for users who want to travel across the US by car with their dog. My role was to conduct a full UX case study and work through all the components of the UI design process to produce a final responsive product. My process included conducting a competitor analysis, user research, and develpment of user personas, user flows, sketches, and wireframes. I conduced multiple rounds of user testing and implimented final visual creative.
