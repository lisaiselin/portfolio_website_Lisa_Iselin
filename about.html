My Story

Hi there, I’m Lisa! I’m a UI Designer, Graphic Designer, and Art Director with over 10 years of experience, living in Colorado. I’m a great collaborator, love to problem solve, and appreciate feedback with open arms. I’m passionate about being that person who understands others and interprets their needs and wants into practical ideas and charming aesthetics on screen.

Born and raised in Wisconsin, I grew up with typical, humble, midwest values. I approach life with the idea that there is opportunity to learn something in every situation, every single day. A self-proclaimed misfit, I guzzle tea, wake up every morning before the sun, have an uncontrollable urge to awkwardly dance when I hear music, and nerd out on making lists. If I’m not behind a computer, I’m probably doing something outside with family and friends, or sitting as close as possible to a window with a book.
