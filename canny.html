Responsive UI Design

CANNY

Canny was developed to provide users with a simple and easy way to manage their finances, save money towards specific goals with one or more “Ways To Save”, budget, and plan for future events and expenses. My role was the user interface design for mobile, tablet, and desktop, producing a final responsive product, using Adobe XD, Photoshop, and Illustrator. My process included research, development of information architechure, sketches, and wireframes. I created the final visual design work, conducting A/B preference testing, logo development, and a responsive layout for multiple breakpoints.
